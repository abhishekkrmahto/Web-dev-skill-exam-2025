Book Name:- RICH DAD POOR DAD

About Book

Rich Dad Poor Dad by Robert Kiyosaki contrasts two father figures—his educated but financially struggling real dad (Poor Dad) and his best friend's less-educated but wealthy entrepreneur father (Rich Dad)—to teach lessons on building wealth, emphasizing that the rich don't work for money but make money work for them through financial literacy, asset building (businesses, real estate, stocks), and strategic investing, rather than focusing solely on job security and earning a high salary. The book highlights the crucial difference between assets (put money in your pocket) and liabilities (take money out) and urges readers to "work to learn" skills, not just for a paycheck, to escape the "rat race" of working for money
